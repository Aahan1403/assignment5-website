About Me

A quick intro and what I’m focused on right now.

Hi, I’m Aahan Bajaj — a business and technology student at Indiana University’s Kelley School of Business. I’m pursuing my MS in Information Systems and previously completed my BS in Business with majors in Management, International Business, Marketing, and Professional Sales. I enjoy bridging strategy and systems to deliver clear, measurable outcomes.

I’ve developed a foundation in data modeling, Python, SQL, HTML/CSS, and Excel, and I’m comfortable translating business goals into practical deliverables — from structured documentation to polished web assets. I value clean structure, attention to detail, and communicating ideas in ways that help teams move faster.

Recently, I’ve supported sales development and digital marketing efforts in Mexico City — collaborating with operations and marketing leaders, analyzing competitors and engagement metrics, and contributing to e-commerce launches. Those experiences sharpened my ability to work cross-functionally and execute under real deadlines.

Outside the classroom, I’ve served as a Student Ambassador for Kelley’s MSIS program and stayed involved in professional organizations that emphasize leadership, communications, and community. Personally, I enjoy soccer, travel, and exploring new food spots; I’m fluent in English, Spanish, and Hindi.
